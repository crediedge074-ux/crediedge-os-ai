# CrediEdge OS Product Definition

## What is CrediEdge OS?

CrediEdge OS is an AI-powered business operating system designed for SMEs.

It brings together every core business function into one integrated platform, eliminating the need to switch between multiple disconnected applications.

---

# Core Modules

## Dashboard

The command centre for the business.

Displays KPIs, AI insights, upcoming tasks, recent activity and business health.

---

## CRM

Manage companies, contacts, leads and customer relationships.

Everything about a customer lives in one place.

---

## Finance

Manage invoices, payments, cash flow, affordability calculations and financial performance.

---

## Documents

Store and organise business documents securely.

Support AI-powered document search and analysis.

---

## Communications

Track emails, calls, meetings and customer interactions.

---

## Automations

Allow businesses to automate repetitive workflows using AI and integrations.

---

## AI Assistant

The intelligence layer across the platform.

Able to answer questions, generate reports, summarise activity and recommend actions.

---

## Analytics

Provide business intelligence through dashboards, trends and forecasting.

---

## Settings

Manage users, permissions, integrations and business preferences.
