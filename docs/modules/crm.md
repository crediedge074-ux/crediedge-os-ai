# CRM Module

## Purpose

The CRM is the central source of truth for every customer relationship within CrediEdge OS.

Every other module connects to the CRM.

If a business has a customer, supplier or prospect, it should exist here.

---

## Core Records

- Companies
- Contacts
- Leads

---

## Each Company Contains

- Company details
- Contacts
- Timeline
- Notes
- Tasks
- Documents
- Communications
- Financial overview
- Credit information
- AI summary

---

## Timeline

Every important event is recorded chronologically.

Examples:

- Customer created
- Call logged
- Email sent
- Document uploaded
- Invoice paid
- Credit report generated
- AI recommendation created

The timeline becomes the complete history of the relationship.

---

## AI

The CRM should continuously analyse customer data.

Examples:

- Summarise the relationship
- Highlight risks
- Suggest next actions
- Detect inactivity
- Recommend follow-ups

AI should assist the user, not make decisions for them.

---

## Design Principle

A user should never need to leave the CRM to understand the current state of a customer.mess
